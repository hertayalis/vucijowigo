David Beckham Drops Brooklyn Beckham Off at College

MEGA

David and Victoria Beckham's oldest son, Brooklyn, has officially made it to college. After a tearful goodbye with his mom, the 18-year-old moved into his new school in New York City, with the help of his super-proud dad. When the duo was spotted in NYC on Wednesday, David couldn't help but take a few snaps of his kid to celebrate the moment.

We're assuming that's where these selfies came from:

Brooklyn will be studying photography at Parsons School of Design, TMZ reports, but he's already started pursuing a career in the field before going to college. For starters, he's shot a Burberry campaign and released his own photography book, What I See last month.

"I’m really excited to start and I feel like it’ll be a really cool thing for me to focus on for a few years," Beckham told People about his upcoming college experience. And although he won't be living with his parents, he praised them for being "supportive" of his passions.

"My dad’s always taught me how to do stuff on my own," he said.
