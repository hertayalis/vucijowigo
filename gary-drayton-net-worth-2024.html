Who is Gary Drayton?

When it comes to treasure hunting, few names are as synonymous with the pursuit as Gary Drayton.

But how has this passion translated into financial success? In this article, we will delve into the estimated net worth of Gary Drayton in 2024.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Drayton was born in Grimsby, England, and developed a passion for treasure hunting at a young age.

He began his career as a bottle digger in the UK before moving to the United States in 1989.

In the US, he expanded his treasure hunting to include beach and underwater searching, using metal detectors to find valuable items.

Television Career and “The Curse of Oak Island”

Drayton’s big break came when he was cast in the History Channel’s reality show “The Curse of Oak Island.”

The show follows a team of treasure hunters as they explore the mysterious Oak Island in Nova Scotia, Canada, in search of rumored treasure.

Drayton’s expertise and charismatic personality quickly made him a fan favorite.
