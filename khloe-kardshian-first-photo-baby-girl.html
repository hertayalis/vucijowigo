See the First Clear Photo of Khlo Kardashian's Baby Girl

Stormi, who?

Prepare to meet the next royal member of the Kardashian-Jenner empire: Khloé Kardashian’s first child, True Thompson.

The 33-year-old reality TV star and Good American designer gave birth to her baby girl with Tristan Thomspson last month and now, she’s introducing her spawn to the world the way Rafiki did Simba, except on Instagram. Reminder: This all comes amid allegations that Thompson cheated on Khloé with multiple women.

While we received a glimpse of True's arm resting on mom's chest in a filter-filled photo on Snapchat earlier this week, Khloé shared a new post on Saturday that gave us a clear look at the newborn. In her debut video, little True looks like, you know, a baby, meaning she’s cute, adorable, and doesn’t have a clue of what’s going on. Mom Khloé wrote as the caption, "Happy One Month True," celebrating her milestone.

“Happy one month old, mama,” Kardashian says in the video. “I love you, pretty girl.”

Khloé confirmed her pregnancy in December, also on Instagram, with a photo of Thompson caressing her baby bump. “My greatest dream realized! We are having a baby! I had been waiting and wondering but God had a plan all along,” she wrote, later clarifying why she chose to stay mum on her pregnancy for months.

“Even though expecting a baby is SO exciting, the first trimester can be the worst. The worst part was keeping this major secret from my family, though! I’m with my sisters pretty much every single day, so it was hard not to be able to say why I was so sick or couldn’t do certain things. We wanted to tell everybody at one time, which made things even more tricky—how do you get all those people together in one room?!—but it was amazing when we finally did,” she shared.

"Keeping the secret from the outside world wasn't as big of a deal for me," she explained. "I believe there are certain things that need to be held private and for yourself. It was beautiful to have something that was just ours."

The birth of her first child marks the end of an era. Khloé and little sister Kylie Jenner were pregnant together, while Kim Kardashian and Kanye West’s surrogate carried their third child. Now, the family has officially welcomed Chicago West and Stormi Webster, plus True Thompson.
