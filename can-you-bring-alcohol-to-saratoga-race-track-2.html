Can you bring alcohol to Saratoga race track?

Bring your Cooler Coolers are allowed at the Saratoga Race Course. You can bring your own drinks, including alcohol, but no glass is allowed.Click to see full answer. Correspondingly, can you bring a cooler to Saratoga race track?Yes, you can bring all the food and drink you want! However, coolers (max size 12″ x 18″) are only permitted on the trackside apron and at the Top of the Stretch. Larger coolers are also allowed in the backyard picnic area.One may also ask, are dogs allowed at the Saratoga race track? NYRA also offers accessible seating options.
