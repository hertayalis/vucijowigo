Mandy Moore Marries Taylor Goldsmith in a Secret Wedding

Mandy Moore is officially married! The This Is Us star tied the knot to musician Taylor Goldsmith in a private wedding ceremony in Los Angeles Sunday evening, E! News exclusively reports.

The couple had "an intimate backyard wedding at Mandy's home that started just after sundown," a source told E!. There were only about 50 guests at the secret event, including the couple's families and close friends, according to the insider.

Although the wedding wasn't a big spectacle, Moore and Goldsmith's nuptials were still a stylish affair. The aesthetic was "very boho," with rugs on the floor surrounding the altar and feathers in the flower arrangements, the insider told E!.

After the backyard wedding, the guests went to Fig House in LA's Highland Park for the reception. The food was catered by Room Forty, and although there was no formal dinner, there were a "number of food stations around the venue, including meat, chicken, vegetarian, raw food, flat bread, cheese and charcuterie and more," another insider told E!. There was also a whiskey station and "lots of wine."

Moore's This Is Us costars Sterling K. Brown, Milo Ventimiglia, and Christy Metz were in attendance. Stars Minka Kelly and Wilmer Valderrama were also present, E! reports.

In the vein of their unconventional nuptials, the newlyweds ditched the traditional wedding cake and instead served 12 different kinds of cake, according to an insider.

Moore and Goldsmith in December 2017.

Moore became engaged to Goldsmith in September 2017. The couple surprisingly met over Instagram. It all started when Moore, who is a fan of Goldsmith's band, Dawes, posted a photo of their album on the app.

"Somehow, Taylor saw it and sent a note to me," the actress told People. "We started emailing back and forth, then we went on a date and the rest is history. Thanks Instagram, for helping me meet my fiancé!"

Moore was previously married to musician Ryan Adams, People reports. They divorced in 2015 after six years together.
