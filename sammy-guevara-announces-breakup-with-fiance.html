Sammy Guevara Announces Breakup With Fianc

Sammy Guevara proposed to his longtime girlfriend Pam in August, but they didn’t make it until Christmas. The wedding is off and now the two are going their separate ways.

The Spanish God addressed this situation with a public statement. He made it public that his breakup with Pam is real, but they still love each other and wish nothing but the best for each other’s futures. That being said, they would still appreciate their privacy in this situation.

I know a lot of you have supported us for a long time and we have appreciated it so much over the past several years.

Since you’ve all given us so much love and support we figured we owed it to all to let you know that we are no longer in a relationship. We still love each other and wish nothing but the best for one another. We would appreciate your kindness and respect to our privacy during this time.

– Sam and Pam

We’ll have to see what’s next for Sammy Guevara, both in and out of the ring. He is on a great trajectory as AEW TNT Champion, but his personal life is going through quite a transition as well.

We’re sending our our best to Sammy and Pam from everyone here at Ringside News.
