Early Life and Education

Lawrence Welk was born on March 11, 1903, in Strasburg, North Dakota. He was the sixth of eight children of Ludwig and Christiana Welk, who were German immigrants. In 1951, Welk began hosting “The Lawrence Welk Show” on television. The show featured Welk and his band performing pop standards, waltzes, polkas, and other music.
