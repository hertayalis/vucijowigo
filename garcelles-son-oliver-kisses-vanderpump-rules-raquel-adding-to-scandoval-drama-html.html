Garcelles son Oliver kisses Vanderpump Rules Raquel adding to Scandoval drama

Vanderpump Rules, the Bravo show known for its spicy drama, has a new surprise in store for fans. Garcelle Beauvais’ son, Oliver Saunders, is making an appearance on the show. In March 2023, the drama escalated with the shocking revelations surrounding Scandoval, where Ariana Madix and Tom Sandoval split, and a cheating scandal was exposed. Tom was cheating with Ariana’s friend, Raquel Leviss. Let’s delve into the details and how Oliver adds to this dramatic storyline.

Oliver Saunders: Garcelle’s Eldest

Oliver Saunders is the oldest son of Garcelle Beauvais. His father is Daniel Saunders, and his parents separated when he was around nine years old. Born in 1991, Oliver is now 32 years old and has two younger twin brothers, Jax Joseph and Jaid Thomas Nilon, who are 16.

In 2020, Oliver married Samantha Saunders, and the same year, they welcomed their son, Oliver Jr. However, the story took a turn when Oliver filed for divorce from Samantha in 2022.

For a glimpse into Oliver’s life, you can find him on Instagram at @iamoliversaunders, where he has around 27k followers.

Oliver Joins Vanderpump Rules

On March 8, 2023, Oliver shared some exciting news on his Instagram. He and his mother, Garcelle Beauvais, are set to appear on Bravo’s Vanderpump Rules. In teasers from the show, Oliver is seen having a blast with Lala Kent and Raquel Leviss.

The twist in the tale is that Oliver and Raquel share a kiss during the show. These scenes were filmed in the summer of 2022, and they promise to add another layer of intrigue to the Scandoval drama unfolding on Vanderpump Rules.

After Oliver posted the Vanderpump Rules clip on Instagram, his mother, Garcelle, chimed in with a playful comment, saying, “Lol keep me out of it.” In another scene from the show, we see Garcelle expressing her curiosity as she asks, “Ooh, should I be here?” when her son talks about having a “good night.”

Oliver’s Intriguing Face Tattoo

Sharp-eyed fans of Vanderpump Rules may have noticed something unique about Oliver Saunders: he sports a distinctive face tattoo as he graces the show in 202This face ink has been a part of Oliver’s identity for some time, as it’s been under his left eye for over two years. Though his Instagram page only dates back to 2021, the 32-year-old has had this signature face tattoo, which happens to be a dollar sign.

That’s not all—Oliver is no stranger to tattoos. His ink extends to his neck, hands, arms, and chest, making him a canvas of art and self-expression.

Conclusion

With Oliver Saunders joining the cast of Vanderpump Rules, fans can expect a new dimension of drama and excitement. The unexpected kiss with Raquel Leviss and his unique face tattoo only add to the intrigue. As the show continues to captivate its audience with twists and turns, Oliver’s appearance promises to be another unforgettable moment in the world of reality television. So, stay tuned for more spicy drama on Vanderpump Rules!
