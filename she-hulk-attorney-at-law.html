Attorney at Law' Latest News and Updates

The Marvel series follows Jennifer Walters, a hard-working lawyer who represents superheroes and powered individuals in court. After accidentally being infused with radioactive blood from her cousin, Bruce Banner/Hulk, Jennifer inadvertently gains Hulk-like abilities. She begins training with her cousin to learn how to control her powers but has difficulty adjusting to the idea of having to become a hero.

She must now try to strike a balance between her work, her personal life, and a superhero life that she never asked for.

She-Hulk: Attorney at Law is expected to be a limited series with nine episodes.
