Jennifer Lopez and Jennifer Garner Reunite at Ben Affleck's Rental Home

Jennifer Lopez visited husband Ben Affleck on Wednesday at the rental home he has been living in to celebrate his son Samuel’s middle school graduation. The couple also reunited with Affleck’s ex-wife (12-year-old Sam’s mom), Jennifer Garner.

Both J.Lo and Garner were seen entering the Brentwood, California, home to attend the family party, per People. And despite their alleged marital troubles, both Lopez and Affleck wore their wedding rings to the gathering, the outlet reported.

Affleck shares Sam, Violet, 18, and Fin, 15, with Garner. Lopez is mom to 16-year-old twins Max and Emme, with ex-husband Marc Anthony.

The three parents also reunited earlier this month, when they all showed up to support Sam at one of his basketball games. There, Lopez and Affleck were seen exchanging sweet kisses on the cheek. And just a few days earlier, the Marry Me star also attended Violet’s high school graduation ceremony with her husband; Garner, of course, was also there.

Recent Bennifer News

Lopez and Affleck, who married in 2022 after rekindling their romance, have been living separately for the past few weeks as the actor films his upcoming project The Accountant 2 in Los Angeles. Affleck has been staying at the rental property in L.A., while J.Lo has been staying at the Beverly Hills mansion they recently purchased together while promoting her new movie, Atlas.

The two are also now selling that $60.8 million mansion. A source told People: “Ben never liked the house. It’s too far away from his kids.” The insider added that the house didn’t meet the expectations of the “Can’t Get Enough” singer, either: “The house is way too big for her.”
