Kris Jenner Grills Scott Disick on Sofia Richie

Kris Jenner isn't afraid to ask the hard questions. In a new Keeping Up With the Kardashians teaser, the momager sits down with Scott Disick (longterm ex of her daughter Kourtney Kardashian and father of three of her grandchildren) and asks him about his relationship status.

"Are you dating?" she starts off.

"Sure," Disick answers vaguely.

"Are you dating one person?" she adds.

"Yeah," Scott answers after a pause. "It's something new."

But nothing gets past Kris Jenner; she already knows who his new girlfriend is. "Is it Sofia [Richie]?" she whispers.

Disick confirms. "I mean, I guess you know who it is."

Then Kris gets to the point: "Wait, so how old is she? 'Cause I really don't know. Is she Kylie's age? Twenty?"

"Nineteen," Scott says. He also added that he's 34 years old, confirming his and Richie's 15-year age difference.

While many would've responded to that bombshell in utter surprise, Jenner kept calm and empathized by sharing a similar story from her own experience. "I was 17 and Robert [Kardashian] was 12 years older," she told Scott.

"You were under age. She's not," Disick answered, referring to Sofia. Burn.

Since splitting with Scott, Kourtney Kardashian has also moved onto a younger romantic partner. The 38-year-old mom of three is now dating 24-year-old model Younes Bendjima. (He and Sofia Richie actually used to be close friends, but apparently "aren't really speaking anymore.")

Not all Kardashian-Jenners have been as understanding as Kris, however. Kendall, for one, has sent subtle but shady clues on Instagram directed at the couple. On one photo of Richie and Scott out with his children, Jenner commented, "Aww, Scott and his kids!" Yikes.
