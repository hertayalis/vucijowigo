Santino Marellas Daughter Arianna Grace Puts NXT on Notice with Lingerie Photo Drop

Arianna Grace was one of the highlights of the NXT women’s division for quite some time. Grace is a second-generation wrestler, as she is Santino Marella’s daughter, and she was on a good run prior to her injury. That being said, she also loves posting thirst traps and did so again recently.

Arianna Grace whose real name is Bianca Carelli, is also one of the most gorgeous women in the NXT women’s division. She is no stranger to making waves on social media thanks to her photos.

The gorgeous NXT Superstar took to Twitter and uploaded a photo of herself wearing maroon lingerie. She looked perfectly natural while posing on a chair, staring seductively at the camera.

Arianna Grace’s maroon lingerie showed off her impeccable figure and jaw-dropping curves. She accentuated her overall look with a leopard-patterned fur coat for good measure.
