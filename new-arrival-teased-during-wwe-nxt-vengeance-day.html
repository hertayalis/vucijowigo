New Arrival Teased During WWE NXT: Vengeance Day

During NXT: Vengeance Day, a mysterious vignette aired teasing the arrival of new character on NXT. The vignette featured the following words:

“Man has three faces… One the world sees… One his family sees…

And the real one no one sees, but reflects the evil he truly possesses…”

Many fans on social media noted that this is a Japanese proverb. However, as of now, there is no confirmation regarding the identity of the new character or who is behind this vignette.

Another exciting revelation that took place was the Dusty Rhodes Classic Finals between Baron Corbin & Bron Breakker and Carmelo Hayes & Trick Williams. The match was eventually won by Corbin and Breakker.

NXT continues to generate excitement with intriguing teases for upcoming developments in the brand. Stay tuned to Ringside News for further updates on this mysterious character.
