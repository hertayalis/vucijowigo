Justin Bieber Was Just Spotted at Selena Gomez's House

Selena Gomez and Justin Bieber are finally hanging out again. The Canadian singer was seen stopping by the actress' home in Studio City, CA on Sunday night, according to photos on TMZ. Bieber was spotted pulling into Gomez's driveway in his G-Wagon about 10 minutes after she arrived home.

The exes weren't alone, however. They reportedly had other friends around for the get-together and "there was nothing romantic about it." Bieber stayed until around midnight, TMZ reports. According to the site, Selena's current boyfriend, The Weeknd, "was fully aware" of her hangout with Bieber. The Starboy singer just wasn't present because he's currently on tour.

Selena and Justin, who officially broke up in 2014, have reportedly started talking again recently, but so far it's unclear whether they've spent time together in person aside from their weekend hangout. This reunion also arrives over a year after the former couple had that explosive fight on Instagram.
