Life After Love Island: Is Jack and Danis NEW SHOW on tonight? Wed 9 January 2019 10:20 H

It’s official. Love Island 2018 winners Jack Fincham and Dani Dyer finally have their own reality TV series!

As if Love Island: The Christmas Reunion wasn’t enough, now Jani’s spin-off series ‘Life After Love Island’ is here.

So, when does Jack and Dani’s new show start?

When Does Life After Love Island Start?

Everyone’s favourite couple will be back on a screen near you from Monday, January 7th!

The show will air in three parts on Monday, Tuesday 8th and Wednesday 9th on ITVBe at 9 pm.

Expect some ups and downs from the pair as what we’ve seen in the press probably isn’t even the half of it!

What If I Miss An Episode?

Missed a part of Jani’s brand new reality show?

Have no fear as all three episodes will be repeated back to back on Saturday, January 12th from 6 pm to 8 pm.

Are Jack and Dani on the Rocks?

On December 6th, 2018, Dani Dyer announced that she and Jack had broken up in an Instagram post.

According to reports, the pair slowly grew apart since leaving the villa and found it difficult adjusting as a real-life boyfriend and girlfriend couple in the outside world.

After all of that, however, it turned out that Dani was just being very irate and that the couple were very much still together.
